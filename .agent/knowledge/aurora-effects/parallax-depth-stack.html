Creating Depth Through Motion

Parallax scrolling creates an illusion of depth by moving background elements slower than foreground elements. This technique mimics how we perceive the real world, where distant objects appear to move slower than nearby ones.

02 â€” Technique

Layered Velocities

Each layer is assigned a speed value between 0 and 1. Background layers move at 0.2-0.3 speed, middle layers at 0.4-0.6, and foreground elements at 0.7-0.9. The variation creates a rich sense of space.
